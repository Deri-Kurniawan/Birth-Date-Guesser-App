How To Play?

  • There are 5 boxes containing numbers that have been arranged from 1 to 31.
  • Then check numbers on all cards.
  • If on several cards there is your birth number, then select it by checking the box provided.
  • Then click the button at the bottom, to guess your birthday.
  • And that's it!
